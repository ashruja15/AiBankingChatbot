WHAT THE RECOMMENDATION ENGINE DOES
======================================================================

1. TRACKS YOUR QUESTIONS
   You: "Can I invest in mutual funds?"
   System detects: investments category (+0.85 score)

2. BUILDS YOUR PROFILE
   After multiple questions:
   • investments: 2.5 score
   • loans: 6.0 score (highest)
   • credit: 1.8 score

3. GENERATES RECOMMENDATIONS
   Based on your top interests:
   • Personal Loan Pre-Approval (because loans=6.0)
   • Premium Credit Card (because credit=1.8)
   • Investment Growth Plan (because investments=2.5)

4. DISPLAYS IN PANEL
   Shows 3 personalized products based on YOUR interests
   Panel automatically updates as you ask more questions
